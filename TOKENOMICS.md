## Risk Mitigation

### Dump Protection
- Team/advisor tokens locked with vesting
- Large airdrops have 3-month vesting
- Liquidity locked for 6 months

### Sybil Resistance
- Identity verification via Moltbook or Twitter
- Minimum usage threshold for rewards
- Reputation decay for inactive accounts

### Smart Contract Risk
- Multiple audits (Neodyme, OtterSec, or similar)
- Bug bounty program (5% of treasury)
- Timelock on critical functions
