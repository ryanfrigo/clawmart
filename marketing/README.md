Marketing copy in this folder follows `docs/RELAUNCH-SPEC.md` → "Trust & wording".

- **Never** claim "how ChatGPT sees you" as a fact. Say: *"the AI models that power

  implies a score carries the one-line disclaimer (see below).

  endorsed by OpenAI, Anthropic, or Perplexity."*

## Firing order & timing

The product is a slow-compounding SEO/AEO play with a few spiky launch moments.
Order matters for two reasons: (1) directories are evergreen backlinks that should
drip, not dump; (2) one channel — **There's An AI For That** — pays a bonus *only if
you launch there first*.

### ⚠️ Decision to make before anything goes public: the TAAFT bonus

There's An AI For That offers a **$300 Google Ads (PPC) credit** for tools that
**launch on TAAFT before appearing on any other platform**. If you want that credit,
**TAAFT must be your first public listing** — before Product Hunt, before other
directories, before the HN/Reddit posts that link to the site.

- **If you want the $300 credit:** submit TAAFT on Day 0, alone. Wait for it to go
  live, *then* start the rest of the sequence below.
- **If you don't care about the credit:** ignore the constraint and just start with
  the directory drip. (Verify the offer's exact current terms on the get-featured page
  at submission time — directory promo terms change often.)

This is the one real ordering tension in the plan. Flagged again in `directories.md`.

### Week 0 — foundation (evergreen, low-risk)
1. **TAAFT first** (if pursuing the credit) — submit, wait for live.
2. **Directory drip:** submit 1–2 directories/day from `directories.md`, not all at
   once (looks organic, avoids spam heuristics, spreads the backlink profile).
   Prioritize free + do-follow first.
3. **Start Reddit karma-building — no links.** Begin answering "how do I show up in
   ChatGPT answers?" threads with the genuinely-useful comment drafts in `reddit.md`.
   **Hard caveat: 30 days of unbranded, helpful participation before *any* branded
   mention or link.** New accounts that drop links get shadow-removed and can burn the
   subreddit for you permanently. Karma and history first.
4. Stand up the `/methodology` page and make sure the free check + report funnel
   completes reliably in prod (test mode) — every launch below points traffic at it.

### Weeks 1–2 — warm-up + evidence
5. **Listicle outreach** (`listicle-outreach.md`) — start emailing roundup authors.
   These have long lead times (editors update quarterly), so start early. A live
   free tool + transcript evidence is the hook; a PH badge later strengthens follow-ups.
6. Keep the waitlist growing via the report page's "monthly fix drops" capture and any
   SEO/AEO content. **Product Hunt wants a warm list to mobilize on launch morning.**

### Week 2–3 — Show HN (one shot)
7. **Fire Show HN** (`show-hn.md`) once the funnel reliably completes a report end to
   end. HN is a single, unrepeatable shot and the audience is allergic to hype —
   go when the methodology page and transcript appendix are live and you can defend
   every number. Best on a weekday morning US-Eastern. Be present in the thread all day.

### Week 3–4 — Product Hunt + X
8. **Product Hunt launch** (`product-hunt.md`) after the waitlist is warm enough to
   drive early upvotes. Launch 00:01 PT. Post the **X launch thread** (`x-thread.md`)
   the same morning and reply to the PH thread all day.

### Day 30+ — Reddit branded
9. Only after ~30 days of unbranded karma: post the two tool-first drafts in
   `reddit.md` to the relevant subreddits (read each sub's self-promo rules first;
   several require a ratio like 9 value posts : 1 promo). Lead with the free tool and
   the methodology, not the $49.

### Ongoing
- Sprinkle the 3 standalone tweets between launches.
- Re-pitch listicle authors after PH with the "now featured on PH / N kits sold" update.
- Only build the recurring "monthly fix drops" tier after **≥25 waitlist signups or
  ≥10 kit sales** (per spec) — don't market a subscription that doesn't exist yet.

## What success looks like (honest, from the spec)

Launch traffic across TAAFT + PH + Reddit + HN ≈ **700–6,000 visits in weeks 1–4**.
~15% run the free check; **2–5% of checks convert to a $49 kit** ⇒ first-month
revenue ≈ **$100–1,300**. That validates the funnel; it does not pay rent. The
compounding engine is directories + SEO/AEO pages + (if validated) the monthly tier.
Market the free check hard; let the $49 sell itself off the evidence.
