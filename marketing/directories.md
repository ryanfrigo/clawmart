# Directory submissions — product-led discovery

Submission-ready listings for the founder to paste manually. This is channel #3 (see
`README.md`): low effort, one-time, evergreen. It won't move the needle alone — it's a steady
trickle of intent traffic plus do-follow backlinks that also feed the SEO engine (`seo-plan.md`).

Verified current in 2026 via research. **Directory terms and pricing drift — confirm on the
submission page at the moment you submit.** Submit **1–2 per day, not in a same-day dump** — a
natural drip looks organic and spreads the backlink profile; a burst can trip spam heuristics.

The best-fit directories are the **OpenClaw-native** ones (allclaw.org, aiagentstore.ai) because
their audience already runs OpenClaw and is shopping for skills. General AI-tool directories are
worth doing for the backlinks and long-tail, but expect lower intent.

## Gumroad cross-listing (strongly consider)

Not a directory — a **second storefront where the buyers already are.** Creators actively sell
OpenClaw skill/automation packs on Gumroad at $49 / $99 tiers today, and the canonical OpenClaw
money playbook explicitly points sellers to a "Gumroad page for the premium version."
[[Gumroad OpenClaw pack]](https://numbpilled.gumroad.com/l/masterclaw)
[[Superframeworks]](https://superframeworks.com/articles/openclaw-make-money-guide)

- **Why:** Gumroad has its own search/discovery and buyer trust rails, handles checkout, and is
  where OpenClaw pack-shoppers browse. It's incremental demand, not cannibalization — most of
  those buyers will never find clawmart.co on their own.
- **How, honestly:** list each pack (and the bundle) as a Gumroad product with the same honest
  descriptions and the same 14-day-refund promise. Deliver the same gated zip. Keep the
  non-affiliation line in the product description.
- **The catch to decide:** Gumroad takes a fee, and you'd be maintaining two delivery paths
  (Stripe-on-site + Gumroad). Fulfillment is a zip either way, so the overhead is low. Pricing
  parity matters — don't undercut your own site. **Verify Gumroad's current fees and its policy
  on AI/agent digital products before listing.**
- **Recommendation:** worth doing after the site converts at all, as a discovery surface. Treat
  clawmart.co as home and Gumroad as an additional shelf, not a replacement.
