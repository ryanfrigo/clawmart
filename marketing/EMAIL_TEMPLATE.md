## Best Practices

### Do:
- ✅ Personalize with specific details about their work
- ✅ Keep it under 200 words for cold outreach
- ✅ Include clear next steps
- ✅ Follow up once (max twice)
- ✅ Use their preferred contact method

### Don't:
- ❌ Mass-send identical emails
- ❌ Use overly salesy language
- ❌ Make promises you can't keep
- ❌ Spam or email too frequently
- ❌ Ignore "not interested" responses

### Timing:
- Best days: Tuesday-Thursday
- Best times: 9-11am recipient's local time
- Follow up: 3-5 days after initial email
